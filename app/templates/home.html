Our Mission

This AWS JobLens Platform is designed to help students and professionals understand the AWS job landscape through data-driven insights. Our mission is to provide a centralized, intelligent dashboard for job trends, in-demand skills, and career recommendations based on real-time market data.

Please contact us for feedback or suggestions at team@aws-portal.edu.

If you encounter any issues, use our Bug Report page to let us know.

How It Works

Login

Sign up as a new user, or log in to access job trend dashboards and insights tailored to AWS careers.

Analyze

View job market insights, skill gaps, and salary predictions powered by predictive analytics and real-time data.

Get Results

Download reports or get redirected to job listings that match your AWS skill profile and preferences.
